# Product Overview

GuardCircle is a family-oriented scam detection mobile app designed to protect vulnerable family members from fraud. The app enables families to create "circles" where members can submit suspicious messages, images, URLs, or phone numbers for AI-powered risk analysis.

## Core Features

- Multi-role family system (Guardian, Gatekeeper, Youth)
- Real-time scam detection using AI analysis
- Family feed showing scan events and risk assessments
- Risk scoring (low/medium/high) with explanations
- Notification system for high-risk detections

## Target Users

- Guardians: Family protectors who monitor and respond to threats
- Gatekeepers: Secondary monitors with oversight capabilities
- Youth: Primary users who submit content for analysis

## Language

Primary language is Traditional Chinese (繁體中文). UI text, error messages, and user-facing content should be in Chinese.
